Websites for small businesses, busy moms, and new ideas

There are many different ways to create a website. The cloV3r way is to let you focus on what you do best and leave the rest to Dave.

  • No Dashboards

    cloV3r sites do not come with a dashboard to manage because Dave manages everything for you. Just send him an email and he will take care of it.

  • Stays Fresh

    Un-kept sites look like a Christmas tree in February. cloV3r sites stay fresh because they grow with your content.

  • Mobile Friendly

    cloV3r sites look great on all tablets, laptops, and mobile devices.

Just Right Sites

Let's not go build a site with bells and whistles that you'll never use. Cluttered sites drown out your content. Let's build only what you need.

  • Splash Page

    A single page for people to know who you are and some other information like your contact details. Start with a Splash Page and grow as needed.

  • + Page

    Once you have more content than can fit on the splash page, add pages for organization.

  • Basic Blog

    Blogs are similar to Pages but with additional features like tags for sorting and comments for reader feedback.

  • Setup stuff

    If terms like 'CNAME', 'DNS', and 'GoDaddy' make you shudder, then I can help. I can help register your domain name as well get you setup with an email account.

  • Photo Gallery

    Have a special photo album shown

  • Flare

    Flare are the additional items that make your site more personable. These features should only be added if you need them otherwise they can be distracting.

Just Right Sites

Cluttered sites drown content. clov3r is Agile by building only the amount of site you need.
Agile prevents spending time and money on features you never use.
Let's build only what you need.

  • Splash Page

    This is square one. A single page to show who you are and some other information like a bio, services, and contact details.

  • + Page

    Once you have more content than can fit on the splash page, add pages for organization.

  • Basic Blog

    Blogs are similar to Pages but with additional features like an over list of posts, tags for sorting, and comments for reader feedback.

  • Setup stuff

    If terms like 'CNAME', 'DNS', and 'GoDaddy' make you shudder, then I can help register your domain name as well get you setup with an email account.

  • Photo Albums

    Splash pages should hold about 12 - 15 photos. After you have more then it's probably time to organize photos by albums.

  • Flare

    Flare are the additional items that make your site more personable. These features should only be added when needed.

"Why not use WordPress?"

Why not WordPress?

Sites like WordPress and SquareSpace (AKA "CMS Sites") are great if they meet your needs. But they can also be too big. CMS are great for larger projects that have a lot of activity but a little heavy handed for simple splash pages with some static content.

Also, a basic WordPress project is going to start around $1,000 so I can get you a good looking site for much cheaper.

Each of the items on the price list describe the kind of revisions that are free vs. paid. The biggest difference is the amount of time it will take to make the revision.
